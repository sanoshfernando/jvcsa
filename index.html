About Us

Welcome to the Science Association at St. Joseph Vaz College!

St. Joseph Vaz College Science Association is a dynamic and vibrant community dedicated to fostering a passion for science among students. Established with the aim of promoting scientific knowledge, innovation, and collaboration, we strive to create an environment where students can explore, learn, and engage in various scientific disciplines.
